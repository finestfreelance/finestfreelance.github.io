Why do I need a personal website?


Personal websites are huge for self branding. You need to be able to sell yourself online, and having a personal website to display who you are can prove to be a huge asset to career growth. Employers love people with personal websites, and it can really make you stand out.

Instead of going with a service that charges a monthly fee such as wordpress or squarespace, people decide to go with me because of the affordability. I belive that paying a monthly fee for hosting is a rip-off. That is why I offer my service as a one time flat fee. We're able to not charge a monthly fee to host because we host using GitHub pages, which is free.

Having a personal website to stand out is the first step in landing that internship or summer job. It is a great thing to put on your resume, and to have on your business card. Your networking will propel to new heights, and it starts here.
